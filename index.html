FAQ

Where is my data stored? In this browser on this device.

Can I undo a save? Yes. An Undo toast appears right after saving.

Can I remove a workout? Yes, but the app will ask you to confirm first.

Keyboard note: all major actions can be reached by tabbing through buttons, links, fields, and controls.

Delete this workout?

You are about to remove this workout from your saved history.

This action cannot be undone after you confirm delete. If you might still need the record, choose Cancel and review it first.
